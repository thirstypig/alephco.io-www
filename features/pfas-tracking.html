PFAS Module

PFAS compliance across 15 states — tracked automatically

PFAS laws vary by state, product category, and threshold. Aleph covers 15 states with supplier questionnaire generation, a state compliance matrix, per-state TOF threshold tracking, and a curated regulatory news feed — so you always know where you stand.

The Problem

PFAS regulation is a moving target

15 states, 15 different laws

Maine, Minnesota, California, New York, Washington — each state has its own PFAS restrictions with different effective dates, product categories, and thresholds.

Invisible chemicals

PFAS compounds are used in coatings, textiles, packaging, and cookware. Your products may contain PFAS from manufacturing processes you don’t directly control.

New laws every year

PFAS regulations are expanding rapidly. What was compliant last year may not be this year. Keeping up manually is a full-time job.

The Solution

One dashboard for every state

Aleph maintains a database of PFAS regulations across all 15 states with active laws. Tell us what you sell and where — we tell you what applies.

State compliance matrix

See your per-state compliance status at a glance. 15 states mapped by product category, effective date, TOF threshold, and enforcement status — with a clear compliant/non-compliant indicator for each.

Supplier questionnaire generator

Generate PFAS questionnaires based on the AFIRM RSL template with 8 core questions plus state-specific add-ons. Send to suppliers, track responses, and flag products with intentionally added PFAS or elevated TOF levels.

Per-state TOF threshold tracking

Record total organic fluorine test results by component. Aleph compares results against each state’s specific threshold and flags products that exceed limits in any distribution state.

Features

PFAS compliance, simplified

15-state coverage

Maine, Minnesota, California, New York, Washington, and 10 more. Enter your product category and distribution states — Aleph instantly shows which PFAS laws apply, their effective dates, and what documentation you need.

Component-level tracking

Track PFAS at the component level — coatings, textiles, packaging, inks. Identify exactly which parts of your product are affected and record TOF test results per component.

AFIRM RSL questionnaire template

Questionnaires follow the AFIRM Restricted Substances List format: 8 core questions about intentionally added PFAS, plus state-specific questions generated based on your distribution footprint.

Curated PFAS news feed

Stay informed with a curated feed of PFAS regulatory news — new state laws, enforcement actions, threshold changes, and industry guidance. Delivered directly to your dashboard.

Disclosure report generation

Generate PFAS disclosure reports for each product covering all applicable states. Ready for retailer requests or regulatory inquiry.

Effective date alerts

New PFAS laws phase in over time. Aleph tracks upcoming effective dates and alerts you before new restrictions take effect.

Supplier response tracking

Monitor which suppliers have responded to PFAS questionnaires and which haven’t. Follow up on missing responses before shipments arrive.

Audit-ready documentation

Every test result, supplier declaration, and threshold comparison is stored with timestamps. Demonstrate due diligence when regulators or retailers ask.
