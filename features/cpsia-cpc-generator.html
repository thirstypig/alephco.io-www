The Problem

Manual CPC creation is broken

Error-prone data entry

CPCs require 7 specific fields including CPSC-accepted lab details, applicable regulations, and test dates. One wrong entry and you’re non-compliant.

Hours per certificate

Manually cross-referencing lab reports, looking up regulation citations, and formatting PDFs takes hours per product — time you don’t have.

Missed re-test deadlines

CPSIA requires periodic testing. Without automated tracking, annual re-test dates slip by — and your certificates become invalid.

The Solution

From lab report to CPC in minutes

Aleph reads your lab test reports and auto-generates Children’s Product Certificates with every required field pre-populated and verified.

Upload lab reports

Upload test reports from any CPSC-accepted lab. Aleph extracts the test date, lab name, accreditation number, and applicable standards.

Auto-populate all 7 fields

Product description, applicable regulations, lab details, test dates, manufacturing info, contact person, and certification date — all filled in automatically.

Export and track

Download your CPC as a compliant PDF. Aleph tracks re-test dates and sends alerts before your annual testing deadline.

Features

Everything you need for CPC compliance

CPSC lab verification

Aleph checks that your testing lab is CPSC-accepted and includes the correct accreditation number on every certificate.

Regulation auto-citation

Select your product category and Aleph cites the applicable CPSIA rules — lead content (16 CFR 1303), phthalates (16 CFR 1307), small parts, flammability, and more.

Tracking label generation

Generate tracking labels with lot number, date of manufacture, and manufacturer details. Link them directly to the CPC for traceability.

Annual re-test alerts

CPSIA requires periodic testing. Aleph tracks your test dates and notifies you 60 days before re-testing is due.

Multi-SKU management

Manage CPCs across your entire product catalog. Filter by status, test date, or regulation to find what needs attention.

Audit-ready records

Every CPC, lab report, and tracking label is stored with a full audit trail. Ready for CPSC inspection at any time.
