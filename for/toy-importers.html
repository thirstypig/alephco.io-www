The Challenge

Children’s products face the toughest rules

CPC is mandatory

Every children’s product sold in the US must have a Children’s Product Certificate. No CPC means your product can be seized at the border or pulled from shelves.

Annual re-testing required

CPSIA requires periodic testing to maintain your CPC. Miss a re-test date and your certificate becomes invalid — even if nothing about the product changed.

300+ recalls per year

CPSC issues hundreds of product recalls annually. Many are for products that lacked proper testing or had incomplete certificates. The fines start at $100K.

CPC certificates, generated in minutes
