The Challenge

Amazon compliance requests don’t wait

Listing suspensions

Amazon can suppress your listing at any time if they suspect a compliance issue. Without documents ready to submit, your listing stays down and revenue stops.

Document requests

Amazon increasingly requests CPCs for children’s products, Prop 65 warnings for California sales, and INFORM Act verification for third-party sellers.

Tight deadlines

When Amazon requests compliance documents, you typically have days — not weeks. If you don’t have them already generated, you’re racing against the clock.

The Solution

Every document Amazon asks for, ready to go

Aleph generates and stores all the compliance documents Amazon requires. When a request comes in, you download and submit — instead of scrambling to create them.

CPC certificates

Generate Children’s Product Certificates from lab reports for every children’s product in your Amazon catalog. Always ready to submit.

Prop 65 labels

Generate compliant California Prop 65 warning labels for products that need them. Include them in your listing images to avoid customer complaints.

PFAS disclosures

Know which of your products are affected by state PFAS laws. Generate disclosure reports before retailers or regulators ask.

Always-on document vault
