Enterprise vs SMB compliance

Assent Compliance

Assent is a supply chain sustainability and compliance platform designed for large enterprises. It focuses on supply chain due diligence, ESG reporting, and regulatory intelligence across global operations. Typical customers have large procurement teams and complex supplier networks.

Aleph

Aleph is built for small-to-medium importers who need to generate compliance documents and track regulations across CPSIA, Prop 65, PFAS, and FSVP. No enterprise sales process, no six-figure contract — just practical tools you can start using today.

Why Aleph

When to choose Aleph over Assent

You’re an importer, not a supply chain manager

Aleph is designed around the importer’s workflow — products come in, compliance documents go out. You don’t need a supply chain mapping tool. You need certificates, labels, and disclosure reports.

You need affordable pricing

Assent’s enterprise pricing starts well into five figures annually. Aleph charges per product family and includes all compliance modules. Start free and grow from there.

You want to start today

No demo calls, no procurement process, no onboarding project. Create an account, add your products, and generate your first compliance document in minutes.

When Assent might be the better fit

Enterprise supply chain

If you manage a complex global supply chain with hundreds of suppliers and need deep supply chain due diligence, Assent’s enterprise platform is purpose-built for that scale.

ESG reporting

If your compliance needs include sustainability reporting, conflict minerals, and ESG frameworks, Assent covers those areas. Aleph focuses specifically on US product compliance regulations.

EU regulations

If you need REACH, RoHS, or other EU regulatory compliance, Assent has broader international coverage. Aleph is focused on US regulations (CPSIA, Prop 65, PFAS, FSVP).
