Overview

Different tools for different needs

Registrar Corp

Registrar Corp is a well-established FDA registration and food labeling service. They help companies register facilities with FDA, create nutrition labels, and manage FDA Prior Notice filings. Their core strength is FDA-specific compliance services.

Aleph

Aleph is a multi-regulation compliance platform built specifically for US importers. Instead of focusing on one agency, Aleph covers CPSIA/CPC, Prop 65, PFAS, and FSVP — the full range of regulations an importer faces across product categories.

Why Aleph

When to choose Aleph over Registrar Corp

You need more than FDA

If your products require CPSIA certificates, Prop 65 labels, or PFAS disclosures in addition to FSVP, Aleph covers all four in one platform. Registrar Corp focuses primarily on FDA.

You want per-product pricing

Aleph charges per product family with all modules included. No separate fees for each regulation or each service. Registrar Corp uses per-service pricing that can add up across multiple compliance needs.

You want to self-serve

Aleph is a platform you use directly — guided questionnaires, automatic document generation, and self-service exports. No waiting for a consultant to process your request.

When Registrar Corp might be the better fit

FDA registration only

If you only need FDA facility registration and Prior Notice filings, Registrar Corp’s specialized service is a great choice. Aleph doesn’t handle FDA facility registration.

Nutrition labeling

If you need FDA-compliant nutrition facts labels, Registrar Corp offers a dedicated labeling service. Aleph focuses on compliance certificates and warning labels, not nutrition panels.

Full-service consulting

If you prefer a done-for-you consulting approach rather than a self-service platform, Registrar Corp’s team handles the work for you.
