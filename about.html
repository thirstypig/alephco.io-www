About Aleph

Compliance shouldn’t require a lawyer.

We built Aleph because we watched importers — smart, capable business owners — drown in regulatory paperwork that should have been straightforward.

A food importer shouldn’t need a $500/hour attorney to file an FSVP record. A toy company shouldn’t spend three days assembling a Children’s Product Certificate by hand. A sports gear brand shouldn’t guess whether their jacket coating triggers PFAS laws in six different states.

Aleph turns those tasks into guided workflows with clear language, pre-populated fields, and one-click document generation. The regulations are complex. Your tools shouldn’t be.

Who It’s For

Built for US importers

Food importers

Companies importing food products into the US who must comply with FDA’s Foreign Supplier Verification Program.

Sports & rec importers

Companies bringing in sporting goods, outdoor gear, toys, and children’s products that require CPSIA, Prop 65, or PFAS compliance.

Consumer goods importers

Anyone importing consumer products into the US market who needs to track compliance documentation across multiple regulations.
