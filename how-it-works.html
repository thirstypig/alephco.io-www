How It Works

Four steps from import to compliant

No compliance background required. Aleph walks you through everything.

1

Add your products

Create your SKU catalog. Enter product names, descriptions, manufacturers, and country of origin. Import from CSV if you have a spreadsheet.

2

Complete the compliance questionnaire

For each product, answer guided questions specific to the regulation you need — FSVP, CPSIA, Prop 65, or PFAS. Pre-populated options and plain-language explanations keep you on track.

3

Upload supporting documents

Attach lab test reports, certificates of analysis, supplier agreements, and other supporting files. Everything is linked directly to the product and regulation it belongs to.

4

Generate certificates, labels, and reports

Click a button to generate the output you need — a CPC PDF, a Prop 65 warning label, a PFAS disclosure report, or an FSVP audit summary. Ready to file, print, or share.
