How It Works

Four steps from import to compliant

No compliance background required. Aleph walks you through everything.

1

Tell us what you import

Create your account and describe what you bring into the US. Aleph identifies which regulations apply to your products so you know exactly what you need.

2

We map every requirement

Aleph cross-references your products against CPSIA, Prop 65, FSVP, and state PFAS rules. You get a clear picture of what’s required — no legal research needed.

3

Get compliant, fast

Generate certificates, run exposure calculations, and send supplier questionnaires — all guided step by step. Each module tells you exactly what to do next until you’re covered.

4

Stay compliant, automatically

Aleph watches for new deadlines, regulation changes, and re-test windows so you don’t have to. You get alerts before anything expires — not after.
