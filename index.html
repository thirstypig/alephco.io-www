The Problem

Compliance shouldn’t feel like this

Fines for non-compliance

CPSC, FDA, and state AGs are increasing enforcement. A single missing certificate can mean $100K+ in penalties.

Confusing paperwork

FSVP, CPSIA, Prop 65, PFAS — each regulation has its own forms, deadlines, and filing requirements. It’s overwhelming.

Scattered documentation

Lab reports in email, certificates in spreadsheets, labels on a shared drive. Nothing is connected to the product it belongs to.

The Solution

One platform. Every regulation.

Aleph gives importers a single place to manage product compliance across FDA, CPSC, California, and state-level PFAS regulations.

Fast

Generate a CPC certificate in minutes, not days. Guided forms walk you through every required field.

Simple

No legal jargon. Plain-language questionnaires, pre-populated chemical lists, and auto-generated label text.

Built for importers

Not a generic document tool. Every feature is designed around the workflows of US importers working with overseas factories.

Compliance Modules

Four regulations. One platform.

FSVP

FDA Foreign Supplier Verification for food importers. Manage supplier approvals, hazard analyses, and audit trails.

CPSIA / CPC

Generate Children’s Product Certificates with all 7 required fields. Link lab test reports directly to each SKU.

Prop 65

California chemical disclosure and warning label generation. Short-form and long-form labels with correct legal text.

PFAS

Multi-state “forever chemicals” disclosure. Track PFAS by component and auto-identify which state regulations apply.
